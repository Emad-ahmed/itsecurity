Protect your network via our three flexible and fully integrated offerings

Nexusguard’s Network Protection offering is a carrier-grade, modular and scalable solution, providing customers with comprehensive options to protect their networks at a cost that makes business sense. Customers can choose to begin with Nexusguard’s on-premise deployment to mitigate local threats, followed by an upgrade to add on fully integrated cloud protection to safeguard against global attacks. Service Providers seeking to further bolster their core services and enhance their ability to protect their own networks can engage with Nexusguard’s Premium Managed SOC services to take care of the service for them.
